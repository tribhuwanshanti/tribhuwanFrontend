Our Transport Service

Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Secondary School provides a well-organized transportation network for its students. Our fleet of school buses covers various routes across the city, making quality education accessible to families from all neighborhoods.

Safety is our top priority. Each bus is operated by experienced drivers and accompanied by dedicated conductors who ensure students are seated safely and assist them during boarding and alighting. We maintain strict adherence to transportation rules and schedules.
