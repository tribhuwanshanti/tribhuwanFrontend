A Legacy of Excellence

Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Model Secondary School Kaski is a leading day and government, coeducational school situated in the outskirt of Kaski, the second largest city of Nepal. STSMSS Kaski caters to over a thousand students coming from various regions of Nepal.

STSMSS combines the very best of academic standards, music, sports, arts, public speaking, and training from various renowned institutions. The school upholds over 50 years of history and tradition with a modern outlook.

As well as encouraging academic achievement, the school provides extra-curricular opportunities, encouraging pupils to try something new and innovative, to develop existing talents and to inculcate new ones.

"One goal, one mission. Our children, our nation", we aim to embody and inculcate with Moral Values, Cultural values, Spiritual Values, Knowledge and Smartness, through challenge and opportunity.

STSMSS Pokhara maintains close ties with other schools, national and international, by participating in inter-school contests and collaborating with schools abroad to conduct joint projects that are of global significance.
