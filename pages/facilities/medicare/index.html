Prioritizing the physical health and immediate care of every member of our school community.

A Sanctuary of Care

Located on the ground floor of the administrative block, our medicare facility is dedicated to nurturing the health and well-being of its students and staff. This oasis of care is fully equipped to handle first-aid treatment and minor infirmities.

The room maintains a sense of calm, with neatly organized shelves filled with essential supplies like bandages, antiseptic solutions, and a comprehensive emergency medical kit. Every detail is designed to provide immediate comfort to those in need.

"Our primary goal is to ensure a safe environment where health never becomes a barrier to learning."

Trained Personnel

The facility is run by compassionate staff members trained in basic healthcare protocols. They stand ready to administer first aid for playground scrapes or minor health issues.

Beyond immediate care, the facility serves as a gateway to the broader healthcare system, offering guidance for students needing professional medical attention. We foster a culture of safety where every student feels secure and cared for.
