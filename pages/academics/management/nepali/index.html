Objectives of the Program

The Nepali Medium Management stream offers students a comprehensive understanding of business operations while catering to their linguistic comfort. We focus on accounting principles, business studies, and economics.

Our goal is to prepare students for undergraduate studies in business and to give them the tools to succeed in the local job market or entrepreneurial ventures.

Why choose Nepali Medium?

  • Better conceptual clarity in native language
  • Focus on local business context
  • Supportive learning environment
