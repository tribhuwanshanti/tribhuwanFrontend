About Computer Science

In the peaceful area of Pokhara-30, Khudi, the +2 Computer Science program at STSSS offers a well-rounded and challenging curriculum designed to prepare students for the rapidly evolving field of computer science. The program covers fundamental and advanced topics, including programming, data structures, algorithms, computer networks, and database management, ensuring a well-rounded understanding of the field. Experienced and dedicated faculty members bring industry expertise and academic knowledge to the classroom, providing personalized guidance to each student. Advanced ICT-based teaching methodologies, state-of-the-art technology, and practical experiences through labs and projects create an interactive and engaging learning environment, equipping students with the skills needed for higher education and careers in technology.
